 IU Connect

A comprehensive digital platform specifically designed to serve the student community. IU Connect provides a centralized hub for students to buy and sell items, form groups for shared interests or logistics, and report lost or found items.

 Features

* **🛒 Marketplace (السوق):** A dedicated space for students to buy, sell, or trade items (books, electronics, furniture, etc.) safely within the university community.
* **👥 Groups & Coordination (المجموعات):** Allows students to create or join groups for carpooling, study sessions, or sharing expenses (e.g., shared appliances).
* **🔍 Lost & Found (المفقودات والموجودات):** A streamlined system to help students report lost items or announce found belongings to facilitate their return.
* **📱 WhatsApp Integration:** Direct and easy communication between users via WhatsApp with one click.
* **⚡ Real-time Search:** Smart filtering and search functionality across all categories.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router), React
* **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
* **Backend & Database:** [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) (PostgreSQL, Authentication, Storage)
* **Deployment:** [Vercel](https://vercel.com/)
